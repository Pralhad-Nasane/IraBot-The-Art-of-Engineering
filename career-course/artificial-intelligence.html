What is Artificial Intelligence?

Implementing human intelligence in machine that are programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions

What this course includes?

This is project based course in which student would start learning journey from basics and will do AI based projects.

Applications of AI:

  1. Healthcare:
    To build sophisticated machines that can detect diseases and identify cancer cells. Artificial Intelligence can help analyze chronic conditions with lab and other medical data to ensure early diagnosis. AI uses the combination of historical data and medical intelligence for the discovery of new drugs.
  2. E-commerce Websites:
    Recommendation system on amazon or other ecommerce websites is the best example of AI.
  3. Chatbots:
    Virtual shopping assistance and chatbots helps to improve the user experience and shopping.
  4. Agriculture:
    Artificial Intelligence is used to identify defects and nutrient deficiencies in the soil. This is done using computer vision, robotics, and machine learning applications, AI can analyze where weeds are growing. AI bots can help to harvest crops at a higher volume and faster pace than human laborers.
  5. Facial Recognition:
    We are using mobile phone everyday where we are using facial recognition-based phone lock.
  6. Robotics:
    Carrying goods in hospitals, factories, and warehouses Cleaning offices and large equipment Inventory management
  7. And much more applications.
