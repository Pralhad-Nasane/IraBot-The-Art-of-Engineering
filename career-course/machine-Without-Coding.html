What is robotics?

When we heard the term robotics, some may think that robot means a machine that looks like a machine, but that is not so true. Every machine which humans can control is what we can be called as a robot.

Robotics is the field of present and future. Even daily we are wholly surrounded by robotics and machinery. The best example is our mobile phone.
